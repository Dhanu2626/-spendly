Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, and login credentials, along with the expense data you enter — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We also automatically collect basic usage information, such as pages visited and actions taken within the Service, to help us maintain and improve it.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect to operate the Service, including displaying your expense history, generating summaries and reports, authenticating your account, and responding to support requests. We do not use your financial data for advertising purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored in our database and protected with reasonable technical safeguards, such as password hashing and access controls. No method of electronic storage is completely secure, and while we work to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third Party Services

We do not sell or rent your personal or financial information to third parties. We may share information with service providers who help us operate Spendly, such as hosting and infrastructure providers, or when required to comply with applicable law. These providers are only given the access necessary to perform their services.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, you can reach us at support@spendly.app.
